What is KnowYourHair?
KnowYourHair is an AI-powered platform that helps you understand your hair fall risk, provides personalized prevention tips, and recommends dermatologist-approved products for your specific hair needs.
Is KnowYourHair backed by science?
Yes! Our prediction system is based on machine learning models trained on clinical data and reviewed by certified dermatologists. All recommendations are backed by scientific research and expert approval.
How do I get started?
Simply create an account, complete the lifestyle questionnaire, and our system will generate your personalized hair health report, prevention tips, and product recommendations instantly.
How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address, create a password, and fill in basic profile information. You'll then be guided to complete your hair health questionnaire.
Can I update my profile information?
Yes, you can update your profile information anytime by clicking on your profile picture in the top right corner and selecting "Edit Profile." Changes to lifestyle factors will update your risk assessment.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.
How accurate is the hair fall prediction?
Our prediction model has been tested with a 85% accuracy rate when compared to clinical assessments. For best results, provide detailed and honest answers to all questionnaire items.
How often should I update my hair assessment?
We recommend updating your assessment every 3-6 months or whenever you make significant lifestyle changes (diet, exercise routine, stress levels, medication changes, etc.).
Can I track my hair health over time?
Yes! Your dashboard shows trends in your hair health metrics over time. Take progress photos in the app monthly for visual tracking alongside your quantitative data.
How are product recommendations determined?
Products are recommended based on your specific hair type, identified risk factors, and reported symptoms. Each product in our system has been verified and approved by dermatologists.
Are the recommended products guaranteed to work?
While we recommend clinically proven products, individual results may vary. We suggest giving each recommended product at least 4-6 weeks of consistent use before evaluating its effectiveness.
Can I purchase products directly through KnowYourHair?
Yes, you can purchase any recommended product directly through our platform with secure payment processing. We partner with authorized retailers to ensure you receive genuine products.
How do I find a dermatologist near me?
Use the "Find Clinic" feature and enter your location. You can filter results by distance, availability, specialties, and patient ratings to find the perfect match for your needs.
How do I book an appointment?
Select your preferred clinic/dermatologist, choose an available time slot from their calendar, fill in your details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive an instant confirmation email and reminder notifications.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
Yes, appointments can be rescheduled or canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without penalty. Go to "My Appointments" in your profile to make changes.
How do I register my clinic on KnowYourHair?
Click "Register as Agent" on our homepage, complete the registration form with your clinic details and credentials, and submit for approval. Our admin team typically reviews applications within 2-3 business days.
What are the requirements for clinic registration?
You must provide proof of dermatological certification, clinic business license, clear photos of your facility, operating hours, and service details. All dermatologists must have valid medical licenses.
How do I add products to the platform?
After your agent account is approved, go to "Product Management" and click "Add New Product." Submit detailed product information, clinical evidence, and pricing. All products undergo review before approval.
